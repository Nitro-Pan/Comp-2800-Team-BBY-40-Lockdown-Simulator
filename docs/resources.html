How to Play

You play the role of an apartment manager, and you are trying to eradicate COVID-19 from your building.
At the start of the game, one resident will be infected with COVID-19. You will start with X action points (AP), which you can spend
on tasks to help your residents deal with the infection on each day.

Action Points (AP)

Each day you will be given X action points, of which you can store up to a max of 20.
Action Cards (AC), which costs AP, and different AC's will cost different amounts of AP. Spend them wisely, as AC's directly affect your residents happiness rating and the total number of infected residents.

Action cards (AC)

Each day will you be given 4 AC's to choose from. At the start of the game, these AC's will be low cost and only have minor effects.
After an AC is chosen, it will be replaced with a new one.
As you progress through days in the game, higher cost AC's will start appearing. These AC's will have greater effects and consequences.

Residents and their Happiness

You will begin with a total of 100 residents in your building, 1 of whom is infected with COVID-19. The residents have a happiness score that will increase or decrease depending on your actions.
This happiness rating will determine whether good or bad random events happen to you.

Random Events

There are two types of random events that can occur inbetween days, World Events and Local Events.
At the end of each day, one Local Event will occur.
These events can range from a simple gift of AP from residents, to residents revolting and throwing you out the building. The odds of these events occuring are directly linked to the happiness of your residents.
World Events take place every week, and you have no control over what happens.
These events range from government issued regulations, to government stimulus packages.
